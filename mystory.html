Mother | Small Business Owner | Triathlete

I married my sweetheart, Shem, in December of 2000 the day after I took my last finals for my Bachelors Degree in Psychology. Since that day we have welcomed six children into our family.

Just over a year before our first child was born we started a business together, Bishop Woodcraft, a custom staircase construction company. Our company brings in over a $1,000,000 in sales each year and caters toward high-end home builders and home owners. Shem manages the day-to-day operations, design, and sales. I manage the accounting and HR. I also provide the accounting and HR services for the Og Mandino Group.

In between full time child rearing and part time work I took up the hobby of competing in triathlons. After my first swim-bike-run race in 2008 I was hooked. I have since completed several sprit and olympic distance triathlons and in the fall of 2019 I completed my first half ironman distance race completing 70.3 miles in just under 8 hours. I have also completed numerous half marathons and three full marathons.

In August of 2019 I began a 13 year dream of starting a Masters degree in Instructional Design and Educational Technology at the University of Utah. Returning to school has been an absolute pleasure and I'm already envisioning all the ways I will use my new and developing knowledge in my future career.
